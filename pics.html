Coffee Americano

Small: 120 Medium: 145 Large: 150

An americano is an espresso-based drink designed to resemble coffee brewed in a drip filter, considered popular in the United States of America. This drink consists of a single or double-shot of espresso combined with up to four or five ounces of hot water in a two-demitasse cup.


Capuccino

Small: 130 Medium: 145 Large: 160

Capuccino is an espresso-based drink, of Italian origin, with 1/3 espresso, 1/3 milk, and 1/3 wet foamed milk. The difference between a latte and a capuccino is the milk content. Capuccino has a rich and bold taste, as any espresso would, but is toned down by the steamed milk.


Caramel Machiatto

Small: 155 Medium: 170 Large: 185

The delicious, multi-layered Macchiato begins with milk steamed until it's stretched and smooth to bring out its natural sweetness and is topped by a dense, creamy foam. Then rich espresso is poured over and through the foam, where it mixes with the milk and creates a brown mark on top.
